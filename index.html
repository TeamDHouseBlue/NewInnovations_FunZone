5. Covo-bot

This robot will do all the sanitisation processes required to keep away the virus. It'll be really helpful if all the sanitisation was done properly and quickly without a lot of hectic.



6. Robo-tester

Another robot who tests you and makes sure that you are safe from any virus or disease and if not, you will be alerted beforehand so that you can take early treatments and get cured quicker.
